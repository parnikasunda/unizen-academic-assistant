UniZen is designed to assist college students by answering academic queries using verified documents.

## Architecture Overview
1. Academic documents such as syllabus and notes are collected.
2. Important information is stored in a structured knowledge base.
3. User questions are processed and matched with relevant academic content.
4. The system generates responses based on retrieved information.

## Design Goals
- Accuracy over creativity
- Calm and student-friendly experience
- Use of verified academic sources only
